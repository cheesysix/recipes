Burger recipe

Description

Burgers are made by filling meat, vegetables, and sauces between two buns.

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Beef patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the vegetables: After washing, chop the tomatoes into thin slices and rip the lettuce into burger-sized pieces.
  2. Grill the meat until well-done, making sure to turn the patty after a few minutes on each side.
  3. Take the bottom bun and squeeze an appropriate amount of ketchup and mustard on it.
  4. Place the patty, tomato slices, and lettuce in layers on top of the bun.
  5. Place the top bun on top so that the filling is between the two buns.
